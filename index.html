3D solver for two-phase flows

  • Unsteady.
  • Three-dimensional.
  • Incompressible Navier-Stokes.
  • Staggered grids.
  • Finite volume method.
  • Time integration - second order implicit three-time-level scheme.
  • Pressure-velocity coupling - SIMPLE algorithm.
  • Interface capturing - algebraic VOF method with CICSAM, STACS and HiRAC advection schemes.
  • Surface tension - CSF with Kernel K8 convolution and height function for curvature calculation.

Validations:

Lid driven cavity at Re1000 Pressure jump inside drop in quiescent flow Zalesak's slotted disc after one full rotation
